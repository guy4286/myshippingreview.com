"What u see is what u pay" is NOT true.

My move from California to London

I'm a programmer from California who thought that shipping my stuff to my new place in England with UPakWeShip/EuroUSA would be easy: I was wrong. I made this page because I don't want anyone else to get the same nasty surprises.

My Story

A Long Story Short

Their price guarantee "What u see is what u pay!" is not true and they will not back it up.  I was charged an unexpected $1,749.65 for a customs inspection fee. I reminded UPakWeShip about their price guarantee, but they refused to honor it (see the emails on the right). Update: I have recently been charged an additional unexpected $2,600.00 in demurrage, bringing the total of surprise fees to $4,349.65.


I originally signed a contract in which I agreed to pay $4,190.00 to move a 20ft container from California to London.

On the 14th of December my container arrived and was loaded and departed for London.  After returning to London I received an email on the 19th of December stating that my container was selected for a customs exam and that there would be "additional costs".  On the 30th of December I was informed that the "additional costs" for the customs exam would be $1,749.65.  After inquiring about the price guarantee I was told that "there is no way we can guarantee these costs into our rates".  On the 13th of March, I received an ADDITIONAL bill for $2,600.00 in demurrage fees.

My container departing, before I knew the extra fees I would have to pay.


Their Emails


After a surprise fee I inquired about the price guarantee and received the email above.

I was told if I didn't pay the fee quickly they would start charging me additional fees. There was no backing out: if I wanted my property back I would have to pay the unexpected fees.

In summary, the price guarantee offered by UPakWeShip is not true and you will probably have to pay substantial extra charges.
